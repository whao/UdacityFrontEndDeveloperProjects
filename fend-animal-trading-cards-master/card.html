Dog

The dog's senses include vision, hearing, sense of smell, sense of taste, touch and sensitivity to the earth's magnetic field.

  • Scientific Name: C. l. familiaris
  • Average Height: 6.3 – 34 in.
  • Average Lifespan: 10 – 13 years
  • Habitat: forests beaches plains deserts mountains

The domestic dog (Canis lupus familiaris or Canis familiaris)[3] is a domesticated canid which has been selectively bred over millennia for various behaviours, sensory capabilities, and physical attributes.
